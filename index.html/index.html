SHAWN KIM Contact Me

I am Shawn Kim, working at the intersection of healthcare and innovation. Currently, my research centers on computational biology and mathematical modeling.

I work as a VC associate at Mana Ventures, an early-stage VC firm. I co-founded dearYou Health, a non-profit, to provide mental health support for high school and college students. During dental school, I served as CMO at an early-stage biotech startup, working in molecular diagnostic kits for infectious diseases.

Previously, I graduated from Boston University under the accelerated dental program, earning BA in biology and psychology. Following my undergraduate education, I received my DMD from Tufts University School of Dental Medicine. Then, I underwent training in oral and maxillofacial surgery at the University of Michigan.
